About the Founder

Kimberly Stancer grew up in rural Suffolk, VA. Her childhood has always included many cats and other animals as well. Kimberly has been rescuing cats for years and shares her home with several of them. She currently works in the medical field but her one true love and passion is cats. Kimberly has decided to fulfill her lifelong dream of starting a cat rescue called MEOW HAVEN. Although she knows she cannot save every cat in the world but she is dedicated to improve the quality of life for the ones she rescues.
