Welcome to Meow Haven

MEOW HAVEN is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization operated entirely by volunteers. Our mission is to rescue cats and place them in a safer community. Meow Haven provides food, comfort, grooming and lots of love. Most of them will have gone through hardships such as abuse, abandonment and homelessness. Many come from unhealthy environments and have medical issues that need to be addressed. We will ensure that they receive proper medical treatment. All will be spayed and neutered to reduce overpopulation. Rescues get to stay with us until they are able to be adopted out. It takes patience, lots of love and compassion to make a difference in giving these little felines a second chance at life. This is why we live by our name: MEOW HAVEN.
